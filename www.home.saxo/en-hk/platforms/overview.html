SaxoTraderGO

SaxoTraderGO

Our award-winning trading platform

Key features
Enhanced trade tickets, fundamental and technical analysis tools, extensive charting package, options chain, performance analysis and comprehensive account overview. 

Asset classes
Access stocks, mutual funds, ETFs, bonds, forex, CFDs, futures, commodities, forex options, listed options, trading strategies and managed portfolios.

Interface
Supports one-screen setup (chart can be dragged to second screen). Access through both mobile and desktop webapp.

SaxoTraderPRO

SaxoTraderPRO

Our fully customisable, professional-grade platform

Key features
Algorithmic orders, one-click trading, options chains and charting packages. Analyse your performance in detail with our revamped account section.

Asset classes
Access stocks, mutual funds, ETFs, bonds, forex, CFDs, futures, commodities, forex options, listed options, trading strategies and managed portfolios.

Interface
Fully customisable across six screens, with advanced workspace management and multi-channel linking between modules. Access on PC and MAC.
