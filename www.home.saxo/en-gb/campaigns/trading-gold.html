Six ways to trade gold with Saxo

Use leverage to trade on the price of gold against 12 currencies – including the US dollar, euro, yuan and Swiss franc – and silver.

Trade over-the-counter (OTC) XAUUSD options to express an opinion on the underlying spot price of gold or take a view on its volatility

Express a view on gold price movements, with direct market access to deep liquidity pools for exchange traded futures contracts listed on COMEX.

Hedge your portfolio, make a limited-risk directional trade or take advantage of volatility, with a range of gold futures options available on COMEX.

Go long or short with gold CFDs tracking futures contracts, using a leverage ratio that suits your risk appetite.

Track the performance of gold with a range of ETFs and ETCs derived from the price of the underlying commodity on physical or futures markets.
