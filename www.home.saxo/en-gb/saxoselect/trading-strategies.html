What is a Trading Strategy?

A Trading Strategy is an investment solution for those who are looking for speculative investments but lack the expertise or the time to implement and manage such investments themselves.

Saxo has pre-selected a small number of Trading Strategies ran by expert strategy managers exclusively selected by Saxo, whom we call Strategy Providers. Using these Strategies we have designed a fully-automated solution for you to invest alongside your chosen strategy (or strategies) and keep you in control in a fully transparent way.

FAQ

  • Q

    "How do I start investing in a Trading Strategy?"

    A

    Investing in a Trading Strategy is like investing in a fund: pick your strategy within the trading platform, click Invest, and follow the online instructions to complete the investment. The funds to be invested will automaticallybe transferred from your main account to the newly created investment account when you complete this process.

  • Q

    "Do I need to open a new account to invest?"

    A

    If you are already a Saxo account holder that is not required. When you complete your investment in a Trading Strategy, a sub-account dedicated to that strategy will automatically be created under your existing Saxo account.

  • Q

    "Are Trading Strategies available for everyone?"

    A

    Trading Strategies are classified as a high risk investment proposition and as an investor you need to show that you are a suitable investor for the service by taking a suitability test before you can begin trading.

  • Q

    "Can I trust the performance statistics of the strategies?"

    A

    The statistics describing each strategy are based on actual trading data from real money accounts. Performance returns and drawdown statistics, for example, are calculated on the account value, thereby taking into consideration both closed and open trades.
