About Michael O’Neill

Michael O’Neill is an experiences and versatile FX professional based in Toronto. O’Neill has over 30 years of FX trading, sales, portfolio management, risk analysis, derivative, and trade strategy experience earned while holding senior positions at major global banks.  He currently manages FX risk for a select number of Canadian corporations.   

O’Neill is the author of Loonieviews’ “Trade of the Day, which focuses on G10 currencies and their crosses.  Short-term trading strategies are identified after a thorough analysis of fundamental and technical factors, designed to mitigate losses while generating FX income. Michael O’Neill contributes macro and FX analysis to Saxo bank’s News & Research Hub.
