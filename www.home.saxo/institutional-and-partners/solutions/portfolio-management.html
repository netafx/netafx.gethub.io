Fully integrated portfolio management and reporting solutions

Our advanced, cost efficient, turn-key portfolio management solution – Saxo Portfolio Manager – integrates seamlessly with our trading platforms. Wealth and asset managers, banks, fintechs and financial advisors can use it to trade on and manage their client portfolios with greater efficiency.

Integrated portfolio management

Rebalance across multiple client portfolios using block orders and algorithms, and trade seamlessly from order entry to settlement.

Risk and compliance management

Utilise multiple rules and risk management features to monitor risk effectively and ensure compliance.

Advanced reporting tools

Get professional portfolio reporting tools and reports, out of the box. Generate PDF reports and provide an online portal for clients to view their investments.
