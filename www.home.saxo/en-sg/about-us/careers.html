Careers in the Saxo Group

At Saxo we don’t just offer you a job - we offer an opportunity to invest in your future.

Our ambition to disrupt the world of finance and our success as a specialist in online trading and investment is built on our employees’ talent, passion, and drive to innovate. We believe that our employees’ development goes hand in hand with the growth of our business. That’s why we strive to allow our people the opportunity to work at their best. And that’s why a career at Saxo is more than just a job.

Careers in Finance/Banking

As a specialist in online trading and investment, we have a strong international focus with offices in major financial centres worldwide and a multinational workforce attuned to the needs of private, institutional, and white label clients based in over 180 countries around the globe.  With a career at Saxo, you will be part of an internationally focused team, regularly interacting with clients and collaborating with colleagues across Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and Australia. You will be working in a regulated environment with a strong focus on compliance and risk management in a truly global organisation.

Careers in IT and Technology

Online trading is evolving at a rapid pace. That’s why innovation and development are crucial parts of our culture. We have close to 500 IT employees in Denmark and India who are constantly improving our award-winning trading platform. We encourage new ideas and promote individual ownership of projects and tasks. In that way, every challenge – and every achievement – in your career belongs to you. You’ll also find the support you need to drive ideas to action quickly, so you can deliver results that make a difference for our clients.

Careers in Operations

As a technology-driven investment bank serving clients around the globe, the Saxo Group is at the cutting edge of an ever-changing industry where high finance meets high tech. Every employee plays a critical role in keeping clients connected, liquidity flowing, and information online – 24 hours a day. A Saxo career is challenging but, if you’re up to the challenge, there’s no limit to where your talent can take you, whether you want to excel in marketing, HR, or as part of our legal teams.

Student Opportunities

If you are a student looking to expand your horizons, Saxo is the place for you. With over 1,400 employees, spanning 54 nationalities and speaking 40 different languages, the Saxo Group is a microcosm of cultures, backgrounds and experiences. The result is a distinctly international workplace that includes everyone in a shared vision to change the way people trade and invest.
