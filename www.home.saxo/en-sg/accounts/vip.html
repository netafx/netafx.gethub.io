    Frequently asked questions

    When you open an account with Saxo, you'll automatically join our loyalty programme, Saxo Rewards. Your initial deposit will determine which account tier - VIP, Platinum or Classic - you join. You can then start earning reward points to upgrade your status, and monitor your points balance in real-time, in our platforms. Learn more.

    Clients can leverage our OpenAPI to integrate with charting and analysis packages from leading providers, including MultiChart and ExcelTrader. Visit the Developer Portal to find out more about software integration.

    Yes, a range of algorithmic orders (“algo-orders”) are available on certain products on our platforms. We support major US, European and Asian markets, and algo-orders are fully integrated into the trade ticket. Once they’re active, orders can be automatically modified based on your preset parameters without you having actively to cancel and replace them. Download guide

    Ensuring our clients funds are protected is of the utmost importance to us. When you open an account, youre joining a global leader in online trading and investing.

    Were a financially stable partner that has been serving clients in over 120 countries for more than 25 years. We hold over USD 16 billion client assets under management and process more than 1 million client transactions every day.

    We adhere to the strictest regulatory requirements in 15 jurisdictions and we are regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore.  As such all client funds are protected and kept in a segregated client funds account, separate from the companys own money and assets.

    Through our managed portfolios, you can place your investments in the hands of professional managers such as BlackRock, Brown Advisory and Nasdaq Dorsey Wright. You can start or stop investing at your discretion, gain exposure to asset classes and diversify your portfolio.

    Our global sales teams and top-tier strategists, the SaxoStrats, can help support you in building an investment solution, but as we are an execution-only broker, they cannot offer advice.

    As a VIP, you will have direct support available, 24/7. If your relationship manager isnt available, our expert trading team will be on hand to support you whenever markets are open.

    Providing we have your approval, your investment manager or any appointed representative may deal with us directly.
