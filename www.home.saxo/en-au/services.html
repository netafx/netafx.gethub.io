Dedicated Service

Saxo’s relationship managers and sales traders provide expert support to platinum and VIP clients.  Our senior sales traders provide expert one-to-one support, including commentary on market events, assistance with placing complex orders and sparring on trading strategies and risk management.

  • Junvum Kim

    Sales Trader

    Junvum Kim joined Saxo as a private sales trader in 2014. Prior to Saxo, he was a proprietary futures trader at Propex Derivatives in Sydney specialising stock index futures. He is responsible for order execution and channel market information flow, while supporting risk management for VIP clients’ exposure.

  • Lucy Payne

    Relationship Manager

    Lucy Payne joined Saxo in 2018 as a Relationship Manager based in Sydney, Australia. She is responsible for maintaining the strength of relationships with existing and new private sales clients, specialising in the Australian and International equity markets.

  • Thomas Greenaway

    Relationship Manager

    Thomas Greenaway joined Saxo 2018. Beginning in sales, Thomas moved into the Relationship Manager role in 2019. He is responsible for maintaining existing and new HNW private client relationships. Thomas’ areas of specialty are equity and FX options, CFD’s and Australian + international equities.
