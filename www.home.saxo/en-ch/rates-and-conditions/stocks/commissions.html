Custody fees for stocks, ETFs/ETCs and bonds

For accounts with stock, ETFs/ETCs or bond positions a custody fee with a monthly minimum fee of CHF 5.00 will apply depending on your commission group. The custody fee will be calculated daily using the end of day values and charged on a monthly basis and subject to 7.7% Swiss VAT (if applicable). Regional differences may apply.
